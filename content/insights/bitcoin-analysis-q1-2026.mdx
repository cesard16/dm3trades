Bitcoin continues to dominate the cryptocurrency market. Let's analyze the key price levels that every trader should watch.

Bitcoin has been consolidating in a range between $60,000 and $75,000 over the past few weeks. This consolidation phase is healthy for the market and sets up the next move.

Sentiment has shifted from extreme greed to more neutral territory, which is typically bullish for the next move up.
